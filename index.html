Market Day is a practical Business Studies project where students create and run small "businesses" for one day. In groups, they design a product or service, develop a business plan, set prices, promote their stall, and operate it during the event. The day simulates a real marketplace, with students selling items like food, drinks, or games to the school community. Afterwards, they evaluate their financial results and reflect on what worked well. The purpose is to give students hands‑on experience with entrepreneurship, marketing, budgeting, teamwork, and customer service.
